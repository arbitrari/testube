About

This is a simple web app that allows Tesla drivers to easily access fullscreen media in their cars while parked. Normally, doing this is a complicated process that is harder than it needs to be. Testube makes the process as easy as possible, ensuring you get to your media quickly.

To use the application in fullscreen, simply press the "Open in Fullscreen" button at the bottom of the page. If you are already in fullscreen, you will not see that button. Do note that your car must be in Park for this to work.

We hope to add more services and features as time goes on to further improve the user experience. If a service does not work on your Tesla, that is not within our control.
